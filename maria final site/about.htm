Our Sister

I am immensely happy to pen these few lines in this official website. Maria Auxilium School, Kancheepuram has earned reputation of own through its glorious and meritorious achievement in Academics, Sports and Extra-curricular activities over the past 44 years. Maria Auxilium School strives to liberate the marginalised children beyond the four walls of classroom to discover new ideas and values paving the way to invent new things. It promotes academic regulations in order to make them more flexible and confident on par with standard of excellence. Dynamic participation of students in all classroom activities provides a platform to attain the unreachable realm of education. MAS aspired to prepare the children as abler citizens to take a lead towards a more humanistic future that will preserve and develop strong, moral character based on moral values and practice.

Our vision

We aspire to be a leading, innovative and high performing school for developing competitive and highly productive individuals with a strong foundation built on values and principles..

Our mission

Catering to the individualistic needs of each student.
High quality learning experience based on a broad and balanced curriculum. To empower students utilise their full potential spiritually, intellectually, physically, socially and morally.
